Antonín Hampl
(ČKA)

Authorized Architect working across projects from urban development studies to construction phases, coordinating multidisciplinary teams while shaping clear architectural concepts and bringing them into built form.

Graduate of the Faculty of Architecture at CTU in Prague, where he also contributed to teaching CAD. During his studies, he completed an exchange at Hosei University in Tokyo under Reiko Nishio and Leslie Van Duzer. After early work at Studio Perspektiv, he moved to Japan as a staff candidate at Sou Fujimoto Architects and later worked for several years at Ryo Matsui Architects on projects ranging from the extension of a traditional onsen to the flagship Omron Automation Center. Since 2021, he has been part of CHYBIK + KRISTOF Architects.
